PAYROLL GIVING

ABOUT PAYROLL GIVING

Payroll Giving is a unique programme for corporate employees and organizations that want to give back to society. Simply put it is an organized monthly activity, where an amount is directly deducted from an employee's salary by the employer to support a cause of their choice.

Apart from payroll giving being easy and simple, it also initiates the culture of giving in the organization and instill a team spirit amongst employees who feel linked to a larger cause. It is a great way to demonstrate a corporate's social commitment in an organized manner.

Employees can join this voluntary initiative for a small contribution every month, right from the convenience of their desks. Additionally, Payroll Giving also gives the employees of an organization the opportunity to engage at a deeper level with a charity of their choice- by participating in various other activities related to the cause.

BENEFITS FOR CORPORATES

Employee Engagement and Satisfaction

Payroll giving boosts employee engagement and satisfaction and cultivates a sense of purpose and pride within the workplace

Empathetic Work Culture

A regular practice of giving promotes the spirit of empathy, positivity and collaboration as people come together for a larger cause

Attraction and Retention of Talent

Brands that are committed to social change and community welfare have an added advantage and are preferred by employees

Tax Benefits

Participating in payroll giving often offers tax incentives for both employers and employees while supporting charitable causes

BENEFITS FOR EMPLOYEES

Choose a Cause of your choice

Employees choose to contribute to the cause that is closest to their heart from multiple causes

Simple Donation process

A streamlined process where the chosen amount is automatically deducted from the employee's monthly salary

Tax Benefits

Individual employees benefit from tax deductions under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act for their contributions

Volunteering

Employees get volunteering opportunities where they can contribute their skills and experience the joy of giving firsthand
