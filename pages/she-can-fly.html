FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I contribute to the "Health Cannot Wait" programme as an individual donor?

+

Yes, individual donors are the backbone of our "Health Cannot Wait" programme. You can contribute any amount that suits your budget, and every contribution makes a difference. We offer several convenient ways to donate - through our website, bank transfer, or even by check. Your donation will directly support healthcare services for vulnerable communities across India.

How are the healthcare projects and initiatives selected under your healthcare programme?

+

Our healthcare projects are selected based on a comprehensive needs assessment. We identify areas with limited access to healthcare facilities and high vulnerability. All projects must meet our criteria of sustainability, community involvement, and measurable impact. We also collaborate with local healthcare authorities and other stakeholders to ensure our initiatives complement existing healthcare infrastructure rather than duplicate efforts.

How will my donation to the programme be utilized?

+

Your donation will be utilized efficiently across our healthcare initiatives. Approximately 70% of funds go directly to service delivery - including mobile healthcare units, telemedicine services, medicines, and medical equipment. The remaining 30% supports essential program management, including trained healthcare professionals, community mobilization, monitoring and evaluation, and administrative costs. We maintain complete transparency and publish annual reports detailing fund utilization.

Are there any specific healthcare focus areas or target populations within the "Health Cannot Wait" programme?

+

Yes, the "Health Cannot Wait" programme has specific focus areas and target populations. We prioritize maternal and child healthcare, primary healthcare services, and preventive health education. Our target populations include rural communities with limited healthcare access, urban slum dwellers, women of reproductive age, children under 5 years, and elderly populations. However, our mobile units and camps serve everyone in need regardless of age, gender, or background.

Are my donations to this programme eligible for tax benefits?

+

Yes, all donations to the "Health Cannot Wait" programme are eligible for tax benefits under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act in India. For international donors, tax benefits may vary according to your country's regulations. We provide official receipts and 80G certificates for all donations received, which you can use for claiming tax deductions. For specific questions regarding tax benefits, we recommend consulting with your tax advisor.
